One System. Two Easy Modules.

Raven links passengers and drivers together into a single, seamless transit coordination network.

Passenger App

A simple, dark-first mobile app for students and staff. Open it up, view active routes, and secure your ride in seconds.

  • Tap-to-Reserve: Pick your exact seat visually.
  • Cashless Wallet: Quick deposits and secure ticket checks.
  • Private Calling: Call your driver securely, phone numbers hidden.
  • Smart Alerts: Book returning trips when outbound shuttles are full.

Driver Dashboard

An easy-to-use, high-contrast console mounted on driver dashboards to optimize runs and manage passenger queues.

  • Secure Logins: Direct verification using safe code checks.
  • Live Passenger Boarding: Know exactly who has booked.
  • Safe Communications: Speak to waiting riders with masked calling.
  • Ride Feedbacks: Receive ratings upon completing successful runs.

How Simple Is It? See For Yourself.

Raven takes the friction out of transit. Three easy steps to get you moving across campus in record time.

1

1. Select & Lock Seat

Pick an active shuttle and tap an open seat on the visual map. The system holds the seat exclusively for you for 2 minutes so you can finish checkout without stress.

2

2. Cashless Checkout

Pay instantly using your secure digital Raven wallet. Fares are deducted automatically, and your electronic ticket is generated with a secure verification code.

3

3. Board & Enjoy the Ride

Show your digital ticket to the driver, hop on, and ride! Call drivers privately if you need coordinates, or pre-book returning legs when campus stops get busy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how Raven works on campus? Here are all the answers explained in plain, simple terms.

When you select an open seat, Raven reserves it exclusively for you for 2 minutes. This stops other passengers from booking the same seat while you check out. If you complete your ticket purchase within 2 minutes, it is yours! If the timer expires, the seat is released back to the map so others can book it.
Funding is 100% cashless and secure. You can top up your wallet inside the app using card payments, bank transfers, or mobile money through our secure payment gateways. In our simulator, you can simply tap the "+ Fund ₦1,000" button to instantly simulate adding funds to your balance!
No! Your privacy and safety are extremely important. Raven features private call-masking. When you click to call a driver (or when a driver calls you), our secure telecom gateway bridges the call, displaying a random system number to both parties. Neither you nor the driver will ever see each other's private numbers.
When a shuttle gets fully booked on its outbound route (e.g., leaving the Main Gate for Senate Building), our smart transit scheduler automatically schedules its return leg. It immediately broadcasts an alert to riders waiting at the opposite terminal, allowing you to pre-book your return ticket before the vehicle even arrives!
Raven is built as a flexible transit software suite that can be deployed on any closed campus, corporate center, or residential community. It features easy passenger apps, driver tablet dashboards, and a complete administrative control panel. Contact our transit team to integrate Raven on your campus!
